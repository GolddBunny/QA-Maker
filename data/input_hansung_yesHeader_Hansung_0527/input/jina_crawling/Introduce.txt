/WEB-INF/jsp/k2web/com/cop/site/layout.jsp global_en_JW_MS_K2WT001_S Major Introduction------------------ The Korean Language and Culture Education Major aims to cultivate Korean language educators and communication experts for foreigners interested in Korea, the Korean language, and Korean culture. Through this major, students will develop advanced proficiency in the Korean language while gaining a deep understanding and practical knowledge of Korea’s language and culture.Educational Objectives---------------------- To nurture global Korean language education professionals who contribute to the dissemination, promotion, and sharing of the Korean language and culture in the international community.Target Professionals-------------------- 1. Experts in advanced-level communication 2. Korean language instructors and administrators in domestic and international institutions 3. Promoters of Korean culture 4. Specialists in planning, designing, and developing Korean language-related content 5. Professionals equipped with practical competencies in Korean language-related fieldsCareer Opportunities After Graduation------------------------------------- 1. Korean language education institutions both domestically and internationally 2. Public institutions and global enterprises expanding overseas 3. Korean language education services and content planning/development 4. Cultural industries and translation/interpretation companies 5. Graduate studies in relevant fieldsCore Courses------------ 1. Understanding Korean Language Education 2. Understanding the Korean Language 3. Analysis and Interpretation of K-Drama 4. Cultural Industries in K-Culture 5.
