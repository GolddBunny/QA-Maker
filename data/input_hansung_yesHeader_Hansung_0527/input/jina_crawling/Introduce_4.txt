Introduction------------------The SW Convergence major is meticulously designed to meet the growing demand for advanced software and AI professionals. It aims to make significant contributions to the domestic and international technology sectors. Through a rigorous and forward-thinking curriculum, the program strives to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also possess a global perspective and innovative mindset.Educational Objectives---------------------- The program is based on a global mindset and creative thinking to cultivate software experts who understand core software technologies and can apply them across various industries.Target Professionals-------------------- 1. Convergence software engineering specialists 2. AI software implementation experts 3. Information security software implementation expertsCareer Opportunities After Graduation------------------------------------- 1. SW convergence development: Samsung Electronics, LG Electronics, Hyundai Motor Company, SK Telecom, etc. 2. AI software: Naver, Kakao, NCSoft, etc.3. Information security software: Public institutions, financial sector, IT companies' information security departments, etc.Core Courses------------ 1. Introduction to SW Convergence 2.
